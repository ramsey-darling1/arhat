Our Core Principles:

Non-Duality

There is no seperation between mind and body, between God and Mankind, between good and evil, between man and woman, between life and death, or between each of us and each other, and the universe.

No Gaining Idea

We are all perfect, exactly as we are, even if we don't realize it. This is fundeimintal. We each already have all of the goodness, wisdom, love, power, grace, and freedom that we could ever have or want, inside of us already. No amount of spiritual training or practice will ever bring us anything that we do not already have. We are not fallen, we are complete. Any idea that we are something less is deception.

No Hiarchy

We are spiritual anarchists. Nearly every religous and spiritual tradition has been plagued by the absues of spiritual "leaders". Almost all problems in spiritual communities can be traced back to a figure-head that has too much power and influence. We believe each of us is our own teacher, our own guru, our own leaders, and we learn equally from each other.

Non-formal

Anything if taken too seriously will lead to a trap, will create problems. Any idea, belief, practice, religion, philosopy, personality, or anything else. It's important to laugh at everything sometimes, be skeptical, think for yourself, and not get stuck in anything.
